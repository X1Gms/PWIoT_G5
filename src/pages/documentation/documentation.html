    1. Introduction

    Welcome to WearWeather, your personal assistant for organizing your wardrobe in a virtual closet and receiving smart outfit recommendations based on the weather. With WearWeather, you can customize your wardrobe, set preferences, and plan your daily outfits effortlessly. This manual is designed to guide you through every feature of the application, ensuring you can make the most out of WearWeather.

    2. Home

    The home section is where you begin your journey with WearWeather. Here, you will set up your profile and preferences, allowing the application to provide personalized recommendations tailored to your lifestyle and local weather conditions.

    2.1 Register

    • Enter your Name: Ensure your name does not include numbers or special characters.
    • Provide a Valid Email Address: This will be used for account access and notifications.
    • Create a Secure Password: Your password must include:
      • At least 1 uppercase letter.
      • At least 1 lowercase letter.
      • At least 1 number.
      • At least 1 special character (e.g., @, !, #).
    • Agree to the Terms of Use: Read and accept the terms and conditions.
    • Click Register to complete the setup.

    2.2 Login

    The login page is used to access your account. Enter your email and password to enjoy personalized recommendations and access your virtual closet.

    2.3 Choose Clothes

    The Choose Clothes page allows you to define the clothing you have in your virtual closet and set preferences for each item.

    Searching for Clothes

    You can search for specific items in your wardrobe by:

    • Name: Search for clothing like a "coat" or "shirt".
    • Event Type: Filter by event type, such as "business," "sports," or "casual".
    • Weather Conditions: Find clothes suited for "sunny," "rainy," or "cloudy" weather.
    • Temperature Range: Specify the temperature range where the clothing is typically worn (e.g., 10-20°C).

    Adding Preferences

    For each clothing item, you can set preferences:

    • Name: Give the clothing a unique name for easy identification.
    • Event Type: Choose the event(s) where the item is typically worn (e.g., sports, business, casual).
    • Weather Conditions: Select the weather conditions that suit the item, such as "rainy," "sunny," or "cloudy."
    • Temperature Range: Define the range of temperatures suitable for that piece of clothing.
    • Material: Specify the material of the clothing item for easy identification.

    3. Dasbhoard

    The Dashboard is your main hub for managing and viewing recommendations:

    • View daily outfit suggestions based on the weather and your preferences.
    • Specify activities (e.g., walking, business) for more accurate recommendations.
    • Access the SideBar for easy navigation.

    3.1 Home

    In the Home section, the user will receive clothing recommendations for the day based on the current weather and their preferences. The user can also select an activity for the day, such as a walk or business, to receive more tailored outfit suggestions.

    3.2 SideBar

    In the SideBar, the user will have quick access to several important features:

    • Home: Access daily clothing recommendations.
    • Edit Profile: Edit personal information, such as the user's name, email, and password.
    • Check Clothes: View and manage the items in the virtual wardrobe.
    • Settings: Manage clothing preferences, such as adding, editing, or deleting clothing items.
    • Sign Out: Log out of the account securely.

    3.3 Edit Profile

    In the Edit Profile page, the user has the option to update their account details:

    • Name: Change the displayed name.
    • Email: Update the email address associated with the account.
    • Password: Create a new password for the account if desired.
